The Best Places to Dance in Nashville After Your Tour Ends

Keep the party alive by heading to these top dance spots in Nashville after your tour with Nashville Experience Tours.

1. Wildhorse Saloon

Line Dancing and Live Music

Located in the heart of downtown Nashville, Wildhorse Saloon is a must-visit for line dancing enthusiasts. With live music, a spacious dance floor, and lessons for beginners, it’s the perfect spot to continue the fun.

2. Tootsie’s Orchid Lounge

A Nashville Classic

Tootsie’s is one of Nashville’s most iconic honky-tonks. Dance to live country music on multiple floors and soak in the vibrant atmosphere of this legendary venue.

3. The Stage on Broadway

Dance to Country Hits

The Stage offers a lively dance floor and incredible live performances. Whether you’re a fan of classic country or modern hits, this spot has something for everyone.

4. FGL House

Party with a View

Owned by Florida Georgia Line, FGL House features a rooftop dance floor with stunning views of downtown Nashville. Dance the night away to a mix of country and pop hits.

5. Honky Tonk Central

Three Floors of Fun

Honky Tonk Central is a three-story venue with live music and plenty of space to dance. Each floor offers a unique vibe, making it a great spot to explore after your tour.

6. The Valentine

Trendy and Energetic

The Valentine combines a chic atmosphere with a high-energy dance floor. It’s a great place to enjoy cocktails and dance to a mix of live music and DJ sets.

7. Whiskey Row

Modern Vibes

Dierks Bentley’s Whiskey Row offers a modern take on Nashville nightlife. With a spacious dance floor and a mix of country and pop music, it’s a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

8. Robert’s Western World

Classic Honky-Tonk

Known for its traditional country music and laid-back vibe, Robert’s Western World is a great spot to dance and enjoy the authentic Nashville honky-tonk experience.
